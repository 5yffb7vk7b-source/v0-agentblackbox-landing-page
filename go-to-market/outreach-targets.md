## Notes on prioritization

**Highest intent right now:** GitHub queries 1–4 (people already merging AI output), HN comment threads (35–37), Claude Code Discord (45), r/ClaudeAI (25).

**Best for volume:** Twitter searches 15–18. Reply to 5–10 threads/day with a genuine observation, no pitch. DM after engagement.

**Best for paid conversion:** LinkedIn (39–40). Senior engineers and small CTOs have budget and urgency.

**Do not open with a pitch.** Ask what their current review process looks like. The pitch comes after they say "I just trust the diff" or "I just run the tests and hope."

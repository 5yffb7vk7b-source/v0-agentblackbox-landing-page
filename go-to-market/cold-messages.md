## General rules

- Never paste the GitHub link in the first message unless they're clearly a developer who wants it immediately.
- Reply to their actual work first if you found them via a public post.
- Do not use the word "solution" or "platform."
- If they say they don't have the problem, ask what they do instead. That's the real research.
- Track every conversation in a simple spreadsheet: name, platform, date, reply (yes/no/not yet), outcome.
